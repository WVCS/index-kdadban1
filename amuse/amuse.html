    What is Fortland?

    Fortland is an amusement park based off the popular game, Fortnite.
    We have many rides, attractions, and food. Go to Fortland today and
    experience Fortnite in real life to get the victory royale. Fortland is
    located in Los Angeles outside the Santa Monica Pier. We are open
    from 8am to 11pm every day.
